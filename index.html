Our Mission

Clinical competence rooted in faith and compassion

SoulBridge Center exists to provide clinically rigorous, faith-informed mental health care to individuals navigating the intersection of psychological distress and spiritual longing. We work from an evidence-based biopsychosocial-spiritual framework — one that takes both neuroscience and soul seriously.

Our clinicians hold that suffering does not exist in a vacuum: biological, psychological, relational, and spiritual dimensions are always present. Effective care addresses all of them. Here you will find both clinical precision and the kind of spacious, contemplative presence that creates conditions for genuine transformation.

We serve individuals, couples, families, faith communities, and organizations — offering direct clinical care as well as professional consultation and training for ministry leaders and healthcare providers.

What We Offer

Integrated Services for the Whole Person

We combine clinical training with contemplative practice — offering services that are both therapeutically sound and spiritually alive.

01

Psychiatric Mental Health Consultation

PMHNP-level assessment, psychopharmacological consultation, and co-care coordination for individuals experiencing mood disorders, anxiety, trauma, and complex mental health conditions — always within a faith-informed framework that honors the whole person.

02

Faith-Based Counselling & Social Work

Registered social work practice integrating spiritual formation, cultural sensitivity, and evidence-based therapeutic modalities. Trauma-informed, non-judgmental, and inclusive of diverse faith backgrounds. Available virtually worldwide.

03

Trauma-Informed Soul Care

Clinically grounded care that attends to how trauma is held in the nervous system and body — integrating somatic awareness, attachment-informed approaches, and spiritual direction to support regulation, healing, and integration.

04

Mental Health Recovery & Faith-Sensitive Care

Clinically guided recovery support for individuals living with mood disorders, anxiety, trauma, grief, and relational wounds — integrated with sensitivity to spiritual belief and identity. We meet each person within their own framework of meaning and hope.

05

Professional Training & Consultation

Clinical education and consultation for ministry leaders, chaplains, and healthcare providers on topics including mental health & faith integration, Black mental health, cultural competency, trauma-informed ministry, and resilience frameworks.

06

Community & Organizational Support

Partnership with faith communities and organizations seeking to build psychologically safe, trauma-aware cultures. Workshops, webinars, and ongoing consultation tailored to the needs of your community or team.

Clinical Framework

Evidence-Based.
Spiritually Alive.

Our clinical approach is biopsychosocial-spiritual: we hold biological, psychological, relational, and spiritual dimensions together. We do not subordinate science to faith or faith to science — we believe they illuminate each other.

  • ✦
    Trauma-Informed Practice Safety, stabilization, and regulation as clinical foundations before any deeper processing work. We understand trauma's impact on the nervous system and attend to it carefully.
  • ✦
    Interpersonal Neurobiology Clinical work informed by current neuroscience — including attachment theory, polyvagal principles, and the relational shaping of neural integration.
  • ✦
    Faith-Informed, Non-Coercive We respect the full spectrum of belief. Our faith framework is a resource and invitation, never a prescription — adapted to meet each person's own tradition and understanding.
  • ✦
    Cultural Competency Rigorous attention to cultural identity, systemic context, and the particular ways that race, ethnicity, and lived experience shape psychological and spiritual wellbeing.
  • ✦
    Recovery-Oriented, Faith-Sensitive Care Mental health recovery is not one-size-fits-all. We integrate evidence-based recovery models with sensitivity to each person's faith, values, and spiritual identity — without imposing any framework.
